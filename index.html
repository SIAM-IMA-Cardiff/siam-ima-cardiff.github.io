Welcome to the Cardiff SIAM-IMA Student Chapter!

The Cardiff University SIAM-IMA Student Chapter is formed by the Society for Industrial and Applied Mathematics (SIAM) and the Institute of Mathematics and its Applications (IMA) and includes students and faculty members from across Cardiff University who are interested in mathematics or scientific computing and their real-world applications.

We organise various academic and social events, including including a weekly PGR seminar and 'PGR Tea', social gatherings and networking opportunities, poster sessions and competitions. Some of our bigger, annual/biannual events include:

  • 3-Minute Thesis competition
  • Bake Your Thesis competition
  • PGR Poster Session
  • PGR Panel Presentation

We encourage all students interested in mathematics or scientific computing and their applications to sign-up for membership of SIAM and the IMA:

  • SIAM Membership - Free for students!
  • IMA Membership - Discounted student membership!

Feel free to get in touch with us if you have any questions or want to get involved in what we do!
