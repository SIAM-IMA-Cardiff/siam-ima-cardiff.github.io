Welcome to the Cardiff Society for Industrial and Applied Mathematics (SIAM) - Institute of Mathematics & its Applications (IMA) Student Chapter!

We organise various academic and social events for postgraduate students in Cardiff, including weekly seminars given by PhD students and a weekly 'PGR tea at 3'. We also organise various events such as the introductory poster presentation for welcoming new students, and the Three minute thesis competition.

Feel free to get in touch with us if you have any questions or want to get involved in what we do!
